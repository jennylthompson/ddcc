Structures: Resource Profiles

These define constraints on FHIR resources for systems conforming to this implementation guide

Provenance of SVC

​Used to assert the provenance of a Public Health Auhority (PHA) over either a Paper or a Digital Smart Vaccination Card (SVC). This profile is not intended to be instantiated directly, rather a Provenance of an New Digital SVC, or a Provenance of an Updated Digital SVC, or a Provenance of an Paper SVC should be used.
SVC Composition

Container for the health content contained on a Paper SVC. A SVC Composition should:

  • ​be accessible from a SVC Registry as located via the WHO SMART Directory for the PHA referenced in the author.
SVC Bundle

An SVC Bundle contains the content to be encoded in QR-code on the front page of a Paper SVC.
SVC Immunization

An SVC Immunization contains the content to be encoded in a QR-code on a row in the Vaccination Events section of the Paper SVC. While this is not a further profile of the IPS Immunization (due to the requirement of gender/sex in IPS in the IPS Patient profile), this is intended to map onto an IPS Immunization.
SVC Organization

Abstraction for an SVC Organization. There should be an SVC Organization associated to each PHA in the WHO Smart Directory.
SVC Practitioner

Container for an SVC Practitioner, which is a Practitioner authorized by the PHA to vaccinate an SVC Patient.
SVC Patient

​Correponds to the demographic information on the front page of the Paper SVC. This profile is not intended to be instantiated directly, rather a New SVC Patient or an Updated SVC Patient should be used.
A SVC_Patient should:

  • ​set the identifier.assigner to reference the SVC Organization resource associated to the PHA via the WHO Smart Directory
